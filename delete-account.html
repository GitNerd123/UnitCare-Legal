Request Account Deletion

To delete your UnitCare account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  • Option 1: Log in to the UnitCare app, go to Settings, and tap "Delete Account."
  • Option 2: Email us at unitcare.help@outlook.com from the email address associated with your account. Please use the subject line "Account Deletion Request."

Once requested, your account and maintenance records will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.
