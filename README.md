# Privacy Policy for UnitCare

**Last Updated:** [Insert Date, e.g., February 15, 2026]

UnitCare ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Data
When you create a Landlord account, we collect your **email address** via Supabase Authentication to manage your account and secure your data.

### B. User Content (Maintenance Reports)
We collect **photos** and **descriptions** submitted by tenants and landlords. This data is stored in our database to facilitate property maintenance and record-keeping.

### C. Device Information
We may collect information about your mobile device (e.g., device ID) to verify software licenses and prevent unauthorized access.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain the maintenance reporting service.
- To notify landlords of new issues submitted by tenants.
- To verify that the app was legitimately purchased via the Google Play Store.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
We use **Supabase (PostgreSQL)** for data storage. While we use industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services that may collect information:
- **Google Play Services:** For app licensing and distribution.
- **Supabase:** For database, authentication, and image storage.

## 5. Account and Data Deletion
In accordance with Google Play policies, we provide a way for users to delete their accounts. 
- You can delete your account and all associated data (properties, tickets, and photos) directly within the **Settings/Profile** section of the app.
- Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed from our active databases.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
