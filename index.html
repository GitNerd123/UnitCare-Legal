1. Subscription Terms (UnitCare Pro)

UnitCare offers a monthly "Pro" subscription. By subscribing, you agree to the following:

  • Billing: Payments are processed via the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
  • Auto-Renewal: Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • Management: You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time within your Store Account settings.
  • Refunds: All refunds are handled directly by the respective App Store, not by UnitCare.

2. Account Deletion & Data Removal

We believe your data belongs to you. To delete your account:

  • In-App: Navigate to Settings > Account Actions > Delete Account. This is an immediate, permanent purge.
  • Via Email: Contact unitcare.help@outlook.com from your registered email address.

Note: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel a paid subscription. You must cancel that through your Store settings first.

3. Privacy Policy (Worldwide)

UnitCare facilitates communication between Landlords and Tenants. We comply with GDPR, UK GDPR, and CCPA standards.

Data Collection

  • Authentication: Email addresses via Supabase Auth.
  • User Content: Property addresses, maintenance photos, and tenant names provided during report submission.
  • Payment Data: We do not see or store your credit card info; this is handled by RevenueCat and the App Stores.

How We Use Data

Your data is used strictly to provide maintenance tracking. We do not sell your data. We do not track your location.

Security

Data is stored on encrypted servers (Supabase/AWS). Photos are stored in secure buckets. We use SSL/TLS encryption for all data transfers.
