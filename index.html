1. Account Deletion & Data Safety

In accordance with Google Play's Data Safety requirements, we provide a permanent and transparent path for data removal:

  • In-App Deletion: Users may navigate to Settings > Account Actions > Delete Account. This action immediately and permanently scrubs your user profile, all registered properties, active tickets, and associated photos from our production database.
  • Manual Request: If you cannot access the application, please email unitcare.help@outlook.com. Requests are verified and processed within 48 hours.
  • Data Scrubbing: Once an account is deleted, we do not retain any personal data, except for anonymized transaction identifiers required for tax and financial auditing.

2. Subscription Terms

UnitCare Pro is billed as a monthly recurring subscription via the Google Play Store (and Apple App Store where applicable).

  • Auto-Renewal: Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing cycle.
  • Management: You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time via your device's Store Settings (Subscriptions).
  • Refunds: All financial transactions are handled by the respective app stores. Please contact Store Support for refund inquiries.

3. Privacy Policy & Data Collection

We are committed to data minimization. We only collect data necessary to facilitate maintenance tracking.

Data Category Purpose
User Email Account authentication and critical system alerts.
Phone Number Optional emergency contact for property managers.
Images / Photos Maintenance issue documentation (Stored in private buckets).
Financial Info Processed by Google Play. We do not store credit card numbers.

Storage & Security

All data is transmitted via SSL encryption and stored on encrypted cloud servers (Supabase). We do not sell user data or share it with third-party advertisers.

4. User Conduct

Users agree not to upload content that is illegal, defamatory, or infringing on privacy rights. UnitCare reserves the right to terminate accounts that violate these terms.
