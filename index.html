Account Deletion & Data Removal

We value your privacy. If you wish to delete your UnitCare account and all associated data (including property records and maintenance history), please use one of the following methods:

  • In-App: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account.
  • Email: Send a request to unitcare.help@outlook.com.

Once requested, your data will be permanently purged from our encrypted Supabase servers within 30 days.
