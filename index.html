Account Deletion & Data Removal

We value your privacy. If you wish to delete your UnitCare account and all associated data, you have two options:

  • In-App: Open UnitCare and navigate to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will trigger an immediate purge of your records.
  • Web Request: If you have already uninstalled the app, email your request to unitcare.help@outlook.com.

What gets deleted? Upon request, your account profile, all property records, maintenance tickets, and uploaded images are permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

Privacy Policy

UnitCare is designed to be a private, professional tool for property management. Here is how we handle your data:

1. Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to run the service:

  • Account Info: Email address for login and authentication via Supabase.
  • Property Data: Addresses and unit names provided by you.
  • Maintenance Data: Photos and text descriptions of property issues.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used solely to provide the core functionality of the app. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

3. Security

All data is stored on encrypted servers managed by Supabase. Images are stored in secure buckets. We use industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit.
