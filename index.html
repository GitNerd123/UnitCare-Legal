1. Account Deletion & Data Safety

In accordance with Google Play's Data Safety policies, we provide a clear path for data removal:

  • In-App Deletion: Go to Settings > Account Actions > Delete Account. This immediately deletes your profile, properties, tickets, and photos from our active database.
  • Manual Request: If you cannot access the app, email unitcare.help@outlook.com. We will process your deletion request within 48 hours.

2. Subscription Terms (Google Play)

UnitCare Pro is billed as a monthly recurring subscription via the Google Play Store.

  • Auto-Renewal: Your subscription will automatically renew unless cancelled via the Google Play Store at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • Cancellation: You can cancel at any time by opening the Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions.
  • Refunds: Refunds are processed according to Google Play’s standard refund policies.

3. Privacy Policy

We collect and process the following data to provide maintenance services:

Personal Information

  • Landlords: Email address (login) and Emergency Contact number.
  • Tenants: Names and Issue Descriptions provided during the report process.

Storage & Security

All data is stored on encrypted cloud servers (Supabase). Images are stored in secure private buckets. We use SSL encryption for all data transmission. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. User Content & Ethics

By using UnitCare, you agree not to upload illegal, offensive, or malicious content. Landlords are responsible for ensuring they have the right to store property data for their specific region.
